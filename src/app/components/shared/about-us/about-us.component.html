About us

  • We started working at Interacto in 2023. The team is composed of: Andreea Scortaru, Flavius Cimponeri, Costel Dobrica & Karim Husein. Our goal is to help people with disabilities and make their lives easier.

  • Since artificial intelligence is becoming more and more present in our everyday life, any new application is absolutely necessary to use this technology.

  • Interacto is built using 2 main artifficial features: Sign recognition and OCR bills. In the future we are thinking to integrate also other modules for people with disabilities.
